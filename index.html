Kenz Coffee House

Where Yemeni Organic Brews Meet Cultural Ambiance

The Treasure of Yemen

Kenz means "Treasure" in Arabic. Founded by Abdulnaser Alnajjar and Moshtak Alamri, Kenz Coffee House was born from a desire to bring the authentic, ancient coffee traditions of Yemen to Canton, Michigan.


We source our organic beans directly from Yemeni farmers, ensuring every cup reflects the rich heritage of the world’s first coffee port. Whether you are here for our signature Kenz Latte or a traditional Adani Chai, you are part of a story that spans centuries.
